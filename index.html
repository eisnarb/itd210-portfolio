What is EDM?

Electronic Dance Music, or EDM, is the umbrella term for electronic music genres originally made for nightclubs, raves, and festivals.

Among the melodic and euphoric sounds of house music, the dark and grimy beats of dubstep, or fast tempos and playful sounds of happy hardcore there are so many genres to dive into.

A Culture That Connects

EDM is a global culture powered by sound, movement, and connection. From underground clubs to massive festival stages, EDM unites people from all walks of life through an immersive experience shaped by inclusivity and shared passion.
